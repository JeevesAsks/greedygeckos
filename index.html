The Greedy Geckos

Once upon a time, in a small jungle town, there lived a group of geckos who were notorious for their laziness. They would spend most of their days lounging around in the sun, sipping on fruity drinks, and talking about their grand plans to become millionaires without actually doing anything about it.


One day, as they were nursing their hangovers at the local pub, they overheard a group of humans talking about NFTs and how they were selling for millions of dollars. The geckos, intrigued by the idea of becoming rich without leaving their comfy spot in the sun, decided to give it a shot.


Despite their reputation for laziness, the geckos surprisingly sprang into action. They posed for digital illustrations, with each gecko providing a hilarious description of their personalities and interests. They came up with a limited-edition collection of NFTs, with each one representing a unique gecko.
